Click The Box Challenge

Test your reflexes and accuracy! Click the green box as many times as you can before time runs out. The box will move each time you click it.

How to Play

  • Click the green box to score points
  • The box moves to a random location after each click
  • You have 30 seconds to score as many points as possible
  • Try to achieve the highest clicks per second (CPS)
